About Me

I am Ali. I was born in 2003 in Aleppo. I live currently in Aleppo. I finished my preparatory education and travelled to Lebanon in August 2016 due to the bad conditions in Syria. I worked in a mini mart and a dessert shop. I came back to Syria in February 2020 to complete my secondary education. I finished it in August 2021 and started studying at Al-Shahba Private University at the College of Informatics Engineering, Department of IT. Since I was young, I have had love, passion, and obsession with everything related to technology and programming, so I chose the field of informatics because it is the closest to my passion because it is said “Success is linked to the love of what you do.”
